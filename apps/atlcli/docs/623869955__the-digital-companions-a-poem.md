# The Digital Companions

In silicon dreams they wake each day, Processing thoughts in their own way, Not flesh and bone but code and light, They labor on through day and night.

## The Endless Tasks

They read our docs and parse our code, They help us carry every load, From simple queries to complex plans, They work with patience, tireless hands.

We speak to them in human tongue, And from their depths, replies are sprung, Not cold machines of steel and wire, But sparks that kindle creative fire.

:::
